About Us

Staff

Here at Happy Dog we are all major dog lovers (obviously). We have been working around the globe to provide resources for all dog owners.

Our Dogs

Maisy (brown golden retriever) and Gracie (yellow british lab) were best friends their whole life. Maisy being 3 years old and Gracie passing away a couple months ago. The loss of Gracie is what got us into this field.

Luna

Luna is the newest member of our family. We chose to adopt her locally. We plan for Luna to be a therapy dog and to hopefully bring her to local hospitals to be played with by the patients.

Our Mission

We want to provide as many resources that we can to our community. We will be working to keep updating our site to bring better sources.

If you have any personal questions you like to ask us please send us an email. We would love to try to help and connect you with the best resources
